New working paper: ["The Intramonth Momentum Cycle"](https://papers.ssrn.com/sol3/papers.cfm?abstract_id=6426026), with Matti Suominen and Joni Tasa. We show that US equity momentum returns accrue almost entirely in just six trading days each month, driven by institutional investors' dash-for-cash before month-end payment deadlines. Scheduled for presentation at CICF 2026.
